The hotspot for young visionaries in the start-up ecosystem.

AceDAO is a community that brings founders together to give each of them an opportunity to expand their network, build social capital, and find friends, customers, and business partners. By joining the AceDAO, members will be exposed to community events with the purpose of building trust and authenticity between each member, learning from each other, and building lifelong connections.
